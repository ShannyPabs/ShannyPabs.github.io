The Journey:

How it all started

Let's keep this brief as Pablo has some gems he would like to keep for his speech! We met way back when, back in primary school (and no we haven't been together since then). There was one person in our relationship very keen on the other, we won't give too much away. However, a while passed and a connection started to build and summer 2015/16 was the start!

7 gelukkige jare

Ons gelukkigste en beste tye van ons lewens is saam met mekaar. Ons het saam ons studies begin en saam klaar gemaak. Hard saam gewerk en baie gelukkig met waar ons vandag is. Sarkasme is die sleutelwoord van ons verhouding, maar gelukkig verstaan ons mekaar goed!

The next 6 years

Since then our relationship has been about having fun, making jokes and learning to put up with each other. In 2019 Pablo decided he needed a bit of distance in the relationship, and whilst we battled through long distance, in April 2021 we were reunited! Pablo realised soon after that Shan was the one for him and was planning to pull a hammy in December 2021, but Bojo had other plans, locking the loving couple in London for Christmas. Pablo kept his cool nonetheless and managed to get a trip back to SA in August 2022. The plan was locked and loaded! Shannon boarded her KLM flight none the wiser that there was a shiny diamond waiting to find a home!

For those wondering Shannon said NO (jokingly) but as we all know Pablo is very witty and has a way with words and coerced her to say YES!
